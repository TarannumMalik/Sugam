About Us

Welcome to Sugam Travel

Sugam Travel Corporation, established in Mumbai, India in 1989, is an international manpower consultant recognized by the Ministry of External Affairs (Registration No. B-1589/MUM/PART/1000+/7909/2021). We are one of the foremost manpower consultants in India, with over two decades of proven experience in overseas manpower recruitment. What sets us apart is our quick response, efficiency, and timely deployment, while maintaining a strong commitment to quality in everything we do. Our diverse activities are united by this core principle, ensuring consistent value for our clients.

We specialize in hiring and deploying talented human resources across various industry sectors. Our core values emphasize integrity, trust, innovation, teamwork, and continuous personal development for everyone in the organization. Our specialists bring deep industry expertise, supported by a global talent pool, enabling us to source niche, high-caliber professionals. We are dedicated to conducting business with integrity and respect for the interests of our clients, job seekers, and the wider community.
